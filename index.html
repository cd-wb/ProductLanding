Blade

The blade of a katana is curved, single-edged, and made of high-carbon steel. It is incredibly sharp and can cut through most materials with ease.

Handle

The handle of a katana is long and wrapped in a specific way to provide a secure grip. It is typically made of wood, and the wrapping is made of silk or cotton.

Fittings

The fittings of a katana include the tsuba (guard), menuki (decorative grip ornaments), and habaki (blade collar).
